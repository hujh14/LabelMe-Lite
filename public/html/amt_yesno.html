Is this a good annotation of category?
Definition: None
Welcome to Training Mode! Use your left and right keys to browse the images. The prediction is on the left while the ground truth is on the right. The interface will automatically mark yes for good predictions.

Task

    For each of the N images, refine the annotation segmentation of the object. A good annotation covers the entirety of the object and nothing else. It is only wrong along the boundary by a few pixels.

Usage

  • Use the right arrow [→] key to got to the next image.
  • Use [space] key to toggle the answer to YES. The default answer is NO.
  • Use the left arrow [←] key to go back to the previously seen images, if you skipped an image accidentaly.

Submit

  • After you answer all the images, the [submit] button will be enabled.
  • If your accuracy is too low, it won't allow you to submit. You will have to improve your answers first.
